Harry's Restaurant

Harry’s Restaurant is a beautiful family run restaurant and beer garden based in the heart of Mayfair, London. Serving some of the finest food in the area, from classic British dishes to our chefs daily specials. We are here to make your time with us the best it can be, so sit back, relax, eat, drink, smile and laugh

Our Story

Harry's Restaurant was founded by Harry Pearson in August 2023 and has since become one of the fastest growing restaurants in London. Harry was taught by master chef mentors all over Europe, with this being coupled by his massive passion for creating great tasting dishes and providing an exciting twist on some classics, has spured Harry to creating amazing culinary spectacles that anyone will enjoy. What followed from this were a series of sold-out residencies to establish Harry Restaurant as one of the most exciting restaurants in recent times. The Restaurant’s permanent home in Mayfair, London creates a vibrant atmosphere that will be enjoyed by everyone and perfectly embodies our values in what we are trying to sustain.
